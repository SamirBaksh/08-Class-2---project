About Us

JustLongShirts.com is a fusion between modesty and contemporary design!
JustLongShirts was founded out of the need for contemporary modest women's clothing. Women who wanted to dress modestly, whether due to faith or personal taste, had a challenge finding chic, modest shirts. At JustLongShirts, our design philosophy is to offer products that combine modesty, style and quality for today's woman.
JustLongShirts is a brand of designer clothing that is chic, modern and has modest longer length women's shirts. We have uniquely designed and well cut clothes for a stylish and elegant fit for the woman on the go! Our shirts fall by the knee and when worn, create the look of a longer, slimmer physique. Our clothes were designed and crafted with the modern women in mind and as such, compliment any figure or body type, whether petite or plus size.
At JustLongShirts, we believe that providing quality products is just as important as providing stylish looks. Our shirts are meticulously crafted and assembled with quality fabrics that are comfortable, easy to care for and will last!
SJYC Wear & JustLongShirts.com is your source for modest ladies shirts of all kinds! Our collection includes dress shirts, casual blouses, tunics, and long sleeve t-shirts.
